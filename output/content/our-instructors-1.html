Our Instructors

Pilates Vita is led by experienced instructors who prioritise precision, care and personalised programming.

Patricia A. Robinson

Founder & Master Instructor

Patricia A. Robinson is a certified physiotherapist and master Pilates instructor with over 20 years of experience. As a member of "The Prodigy," the second generation of Pilates instructors trained by the original Elders, she has learned from first-generation teachers trained by Joseph Pilates.

Patricia has created her own method within the Pilates methodology. She certifies teachers globally and has developed her own Pilates certification training and studio licensing programme in the United States.

Patricia's method, "Conscience of Movement through Pilates," helps clients learn to conscientiously move their bodies, gaining a greater sense of what they are doing with their muscles when they move. They learn how to control their movements, enhancing strength, flexibility, and overall well-being.

She prioritises quality over repetition, ensuring precision and purpose in each movement. She believes that each body needs an individual approach and each individual needs to understand the correct movements to achieve their body goals.

Through this combination of consistent movement and correct exercises for personal performance, Patricia integrates fitness, nutrition, and natural healing for a comprehensive wellness experience. Her dedication to continuous learning ensures she can address each client's unique needs, helping them move better, feel better, and live better.

By educating clients on proper movement mechanics, Patricia empowers them to maintain progress and prevent injuries. Her philosophy emphasises the connection between a healthy mind and body, fostering overall well-being.

Experience the unique approach Patricia brings to Pilates and holistic wellness at Pilates Vita. Discover how her expertise can help you achieve a balanced, healthier lifestyle with a stronger, more connected body and mind.

Suelen Coelho Da Silva

Lead Instructor

Suelen Coelho Da Silva is one of the lead instructors at Pilates Vita. Suelen is deeply passionate about empowering individuals through the transformative power of Pilates. With her dedication to teaching and practice, Suelen has developed a profound understanding of how Pilates not only enhances physical strength and flexibility but also promotes an overall sense of well-being, helping her clients to reconnect their body and mind.

Suelen believes that Pilates is more than just a form of exercise; it's a pathway to a healthier, more vibrant life. Her approach is personalised and attentive, ensuring that each client receives the guidance and support they need to meet their personal health and wellness goals.

Whether clients are recovering from an injury, looking to improve their athletic performance, or simply seeking a more balanced lifestyle, Suelen is here to support them every step of the way.

Join Suelen at Pilates Vita to experience the joy and transformation that Pilates can bring to your life. Embark on this beautiful journey together, connecting mind and body to achieve a state of complete health and happiness.
