Patricia A. Robinson

Instructor at Pilates Vita

Patricia A. Robinson is a certified physiotherapist and master Pilates instructor with over 20 years of experience. As a member of "The Prodigy," the second generation of Pilates instructors trained by the original Elders, she has learned from first-generation teachers trained by Joseph Pilates. Patricia has created her own method within the Pilates methodology. She certifies teachers globally and has developed her own Pilates certification training and studio licensing programme in the United States.

Patricia’s method, "Conscience of Movement through Pilates," helps clients learn to conscientiously move their bodies, gaining a greater sense of what they are doing with their muscles when they move. They learn how to control their movements, enhancing strength, flexibility, and overall well-being.

She prioritises quality over repetition, ensuring precision and purpose in each movement. She believes that each body needs an individual approach and each individual needs to understand the correct movements to achieve their body goals.

Through this combination of consistent movement and correct exercises for personal performance, Patricia integrates fitness, nutrition, and natural healing for a comprehensive wellness experience. Her dedication to continuous learning ensures she can address each client's unique needs, helping them move better, feel better, and live better. She believes that conscious, consistent movement is the key to health and ageless individuals.

By educating clients on proper movement mechanics, Patricia empowers them to maintain progress and prevent injuries. Her philosophy emphasises the connection between a healthy mind and body, fostering overall well-being.

Experience the unique approach Patricia brings to Pilates and holistic wellness at Pilates Vita. Discover how her expertise can help you achieve a balanced, healthier lifestyle with a stronger, more connected body and mind.
