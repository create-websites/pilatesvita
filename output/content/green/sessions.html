Session type

Private 1-1 Session (55 mins)

Experience the luxury of a private session at Pilates Vita, where you'll work closely with one of our expert instructors. Tailored to your individual goals and preferences, this session offers a personalised workout using the Pilates equipment of your choice. Whether you're looking to improve strength, enhance flexibility, recover from an injury, or boost overall well-being, our instructors will provide you with undivided attention to ensure each movement is executed with precision for maximum benefit. This session also offers the perfect opportunity to ask questions and learn the language of Pilates effectively. If you're new to Pilates, recovering from an injury, or haven't exercised in a while, this private session is mandatory before joining any group classes.

Booking & policies

Important information

To secure a session, prior payment and booking are required. Sessions will not commence without confirmation of payment and booking. We kindly request a minimum of 48 hours' notice for cancellations. Failure to provide this notice will result in billing for the scheduled session. Sessions are transferable but non-refundable. All PK packages are valid for six months, with the option to extend validity if requested on the day of purchase, and a note will be made on the client's file. In cases where validation is postponed, the period may be extended up to one year. Clients are encouraged to arrive promptly for sessions; however, instructors will wait for up to thirty minutes past the booking time. If clients fail to arrive within this grace period, their session may be forfeited, and charges may still apply. Please note that images of clients may be captured and shared on social media without compensation. Clients wishing to opt out of this must provide written notice via email. Additionally, clients acknowledge that instructors are not liable for any injuries sustained during sessions, and by participating, clients agree to adhere to these policies.
