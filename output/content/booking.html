What to Share When You Reach Out

A few details help us recommend the right session type and pacing—especially for first-time clients.

  • Your Goals

    Strength, mobility, postural work, rehabilitation, sport performance, or general wellbeing.

  • Experience Level

    New to Pilates or experienced; mat, reformer, or apparatus background.

  • Availability

    Preferred days/times, plus any flexibility.

  • Injuries or Constraints

    Any relevant considerations so we can advise safe modifications.
